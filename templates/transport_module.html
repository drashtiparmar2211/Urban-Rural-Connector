How Dual-OTP Works
1. Pickup OTP
Farmer generates OTP → gives to Driver → Driver enters into app → Package released for transit
2. Proof of Condition
Driver photographs product before loading → uploaded to order record → buyer can view pre-transit condition
3. Delivery OTP
Buyer generates OTP → gives to Driver → Driver enters into app → Payment released → Order marked Delivered
